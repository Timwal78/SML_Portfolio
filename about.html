About ScriptMaster Labs

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

ScriptMaster Labs is a premier protocol engineering and AI deployment firm specializing in advanced AI-native tools, x402 payment rails, and enterprise-grade automation systems.

Founded by Timothy Walton, ScriptMaster Labs is committed to building resilient, trust-minimized software infrastructure. We are currently navigating the certification process to become an officially recognized Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) through VetCert.

Our core ecosystem is anchored by the SqueezeOS architecture, NEXUS402, and the Hawkeye Command App. Our development ethos prioritizes verifiable execution, scalable data ingestion, and uncompromised system integrity.

In parallel with our commercial engineering efforts, our founder leads VA Ratings, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 41-3828332) dedicated to supporting and empowering veterans.
