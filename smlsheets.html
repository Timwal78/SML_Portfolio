Questions

Straight answers

→What is SMLSheets.AI?
An AI-native spreadsheet from Script Master Labs that replaces Google Sheets. You bring your own AI key, there's no subscription, and you own 100% of your data.
→Does it require a subscription?
No. SMLSheets.AI is a one-time purchase. You pay your chosen AI provider directly at cost — no markup, no recurring fee to us.
→Who owns the data?
You do — 100%. Script Master Labs cannot read, store, or sell your sheets. Your AI calls go directly from your machine to your chosen provider.

You connect your own OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter API key in settings. Every AI request in SMLSheets.AI uses that key directly — we never see your prompts or your data.

Yes — CSV and XLSX import and export, both ways, anytime. No lock-in.
