Scoring Model

What We Measure. Why It Matters.

Every TrustScore is derived from five operational dimensions measured against observable vendor behavior — not self-reported questionnaires or annual audits.

01 · DIMENSION
Payment Reliability
Weight: 25% of TrustScore
Does this vendor consistently fulfill its financial obligations? A vendor that can't pay its own infrastructure bills is a vendor that will disappear without warning. We track on-time payment rates, average payment delays, and transaction consistency.
02 · DIMENSION
Uptime Consistency
Weight: 25% of TrustScore
Is the vendor's service consistently available? We measure actual API response times, error rates, and declared vs. actual maintenance windows. A vendor at 99.9% uptime versus 95% — we catch the difference before your customers do.
03 · DIMENSION
Security Posture
Weight: 20% of TrustScore
Has the vendor had security incidents? Are their API keys exposed? Do they rotate credentials on schedule? We monitor public repositories, breach databases, and security advisories in real time — not once a year.
04 · DIMENSION
Contract Adherence
Weight: 15% of TrustScore
Does the vendor operate within the bounds of its contract? We monitor data handling terms, geographic restrictions, usage limits, and termination clauses — flagging deviations before they become liabilities.
05 · DIMENSION
Operational Longevity
Weight: 15% of TrustScore
How long has this vendor been operating? Infrastructure age, transaction history, and operational continuity tell the story that pitch decks don't. A flash-in-the-pan startup scores differently than a 2-year stable operation.
